Frequently Asked Questions

What is an Annual Report filing?

An Annual Report updates the state database with key details about your business structures, including management changes, operating addresses, and authorized agents.

What happens if my business misses a deadline?

States apply steep punitive financial penalties quickly and eventually change company statuses to "Inactive" or "Dissolved"—putting your personal asset protection shields at high risk.
