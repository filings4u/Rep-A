Knowledge Base

Common Questions

What is an MCS-90 Endorsement?

It is an endorsement attached to your liability policy that guarantees payment to the public if a judgement is rendered against you, even if the incident isn't covered by the base policy.

How long does insurance take to reflect?

Once your insurance provider transmits the data, it typically takes 24-48 hours to appear in the FMCSA Licensing and Insurance (L&I) system.

BMC-84 vs BMC-85?

BMC-84 is a surety bond (paid annually), while BMC-85 is a trust fund where you deposit the full $75,000 in cash into a trust.

Do I need Bobtail Insurance?

While not an FMCSA requirement for authority, Non-Trucking Liability (Bobtail) is essential for owner-operators driving without a loaded trailer.
